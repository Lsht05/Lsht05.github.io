Co-author of a SAT Math book

Since April 2021, I have been working in collaboration with Prof. Semere Baraki (B.A. Math, The University of Chicago, MS work, The Courant Institute of New York University) to write a SAT Math Preparation Book. This resource aims to give students all the necessary tools to ace the SAT math section. The book (300+ pages) is in the final revision phase and will be published in the next few weeks. My work consisted of creating problems and writing their solutions, creating lessons and topic explanations, and format editing (such as creating images, and charts, revising the formatting, etc).
