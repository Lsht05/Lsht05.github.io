Guitar

When I was four years old, my Colombian uncle gifted me a small guitar. It turns out that it was a requinto, but no one realized it until two years ago. I enjoyed playing with it, but I never started to actually learn how to play it until 2017. That is when I started my journey as a self-taught guitarist. I learned all the basics with the small requinto until my parents gave me my first and only guitar in 2020.
