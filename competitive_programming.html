Competitive Programming

After watching a YouTube video on the topic, I got completely hooked on this mind sport, and have been practicing it regularly since early 2021. This has been one of the factors that made me realize that I wanted to pursue a major in Computer Science. Throughout my competitive programming journey, in addition to learning lots of thrilling stuff, I also met a lot of great friends from many countries.

International School & Cup in Informatics “Junior” (ISIJ-2022)

Because of my rank in previous national and Iberoamerican olympiads, I was one of the six Peruvians who were invited to the international event. I obtained three individual silver medals and one bronze medal as a member of the national team.

Iberoamerican Computing and Informatics Olympiad 2022

I was part of the team of 10 that represented Peru in the Iberoamerican Computing and Informatics Olympiad (CIIC) 2022. I won a bronze medal.

Scholar Programming Contest 2021

Ranked 4th in the national programming contest organized by the Universidad Catolica San Pablo. In the previous year's edition, I ranked 7th.

Peruvian Olympiad in Informatics (OPI) 2022

I ranked 10th in the Peruvian Olympiad in Informatics 2022, obtaining an honorable mention and a quota to participate in the Iberoamerican Computing and Informatics Olympiad. In the previous edition of the OPI, I ranked 13th.
