School STEAM Club Presidency

Since the beginning of 2021, I became the president of my school's STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math) Club, organizing multiple meetings every week, and guiding the members into developing projects and learning STEAM concepts.

Coolest Projects 2021 - Favorite Project

My School's STEAM club participated in the Coolest Projects competition, developing a program to help people with visual impairments learn. Our project was selected as one of the favorites, among hundreds of projects. My role was to program the graphical interface, as well as guide the other members of the team.
