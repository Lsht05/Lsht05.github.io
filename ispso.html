International STEM Problem Solving Olympiad - Silver Medalist

I obtained a silver medal in the International STEM Problem Solving Olympiad (ISPSO) 2022. For this achievement, first I had to qualify for the final stage, and then I had to prepare a scientific presentation for a STEM problem: How to use water desalination efficiently to fight against the issue of lack of fresh water on the planet.

ISPSO 2022 - Diploma

I qualified for the final stage with first place in the semi-final and then ranked 4th in the final stage, obtaining the first silver medal.
